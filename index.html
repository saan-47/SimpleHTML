Subject: Launch of “Digital Lending – Elite Instant Loan”

The advent of Digital Transformation across industry has led to the usage of innovative means for business acquisition. Keeping this notion in view, this initiative capitalizes on the existing employee-banking (EB) customer data through an instant acquisition process.

Digital Transformation along with Product Team has introduced a digital lending platform designed to cater our Employee-banking customers (with Pre-Approved limits) on the go. EB customers are tapped via SMS with a unique link for individuals to avail the facility and then routed to a Web UI portal to continue the process with zero documentation and no branch visits. Portal provides three pre-approved loan options to choose from and the application is processed in just a few simple clicks.

It is a two-stage solution where the first stage is the front-end, enabling customer to request the loan amount. The second stage is where the CAD user reviews the details and posts the entry for disbursement.

Other aspects of the solution will be reducing the turn-around time, maintaining a real-time trail of inbound applications and swift approvals. JS Elite Instant Loan, a first-of-its-kind product on the JS Bank platform aims to develop a customer-centric framework for instant lending enabling quick and paperless acquisition.
